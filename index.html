WILL I GET 100% OWNERSHIP OF THE WEBSITE?

Yes, the website design is your ownership. We will not use it for other clients, as per the contract. We offer website maintenance services as well to ensure that your website stays up to date.

HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO DESIGN MY FIRM'S WEBSITE?

Normally, a website usually takes 15 to 30 days to complete. However, the time required for the completion of the web project depends greatly on the complexity of the project. Webfinity Inc aims to provide you with an excellent web experience for you and your client at the fastest turnaround.

HOW MANY WEBSITE PAGES WILL BE CREATED FOR MY FIRM?

A law-firm website typically requires four to five pages - Home, Services, Packages, About Us, and Contact Us pages. We can add up more web pages to the website as per the client's requirement.
